# Phishing Email Detection & Awareness

## Project Overview
This project demonstrates how phishing emails can be identified and analyzed using basic cybersecurity techniques. The objective is to detect phishing indicators and create awareness about email-based cyber attacks.

## Sample Email
Subject: Urgent – Your Bank Account Will Be Suspended

Dear Customer,

We noticed unusual activity in your bank account.  
Please verify your account immediately to avoid suspension.

Click here to verify:  
http://secure-bank-login-update.com/verify

Failure to verify within 24 hours will result in account suspension.

Thank you,  
Bank Security Team

## Phishing Indicators Identified
- Generic greeting ("Dear Customer")
- Urgent message creating panic
- Suspicious link with fake domain
- Possible spoofed sender

## Email Classification
Phishing Email

The email attempts to trick the user into clicking a malicious link and entering sensitive information such as banking credentials.

## Prevention Guidelines
- Do not click unknown or suspicious links
- Verify the sender's email address
- Check website URLs carefully before entering credentials
- Use spam filters and email security tools
- Report suspicious emails to the security team

## Conclusion
The analyzed email contains several phishing indicators and is classified as a phishing attempt. Awareness and cautious email handling can help prevent such cyber attacks.# FUTURE_CS_02
